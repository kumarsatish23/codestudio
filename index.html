Printing Services

Custom T-shirts Printing & Embroidery. We specialize in branding onto clothes & accessories. We give the best finished look our customers want.

Direct To Garment Printing

This is an alternative to screen printing or sublimation printing.Digital garment printing has a tendency to print your image directly onto clothes mistreatment permanent inks.

Enquire Us

Sublimation Printing

This is the easiest and cheaper method of printing. This printing is fast and economical but has limitations to print on light coloured Polyester clothes.

Enquire Us

Screen Printing

This method is used to bond various inks directly to the garment with the help of screen under high heat and heavy pressure. Some of the most commonly used in brand logos, sports numbers and letters.

Enquire Us

Custom Embroidery

The most used application which is foremost “classy”.We tend to stitch your custom brand for embroidery by re-drawing it in stitches. This is a long process but the outcome is really worth it.

Personalized Gifts

We have a wide range of customized gifts onto which you can print your names, photos and brands. This brings smiles on the faces of your loved ones. We believe that every gift is a memory of a lifetime.
