Direct To Garment Printing

This is an alternative to screen printing or sublimation printing.Digital garment printing has a tendency to print your image directly onto clothes mistreatment permanent inks.

Sublimation Printing

This is the easiest and cheaper method of printing. This printing is fast and economical but has limitations to print on light coloured Polyester clothes.

Enquire Us

Screen Printing

This method is used to bond various inks directly to the garment with the help of screen under high heat and heavy pressure. Some of the most commonly used in brand logos, sports numbers and letters.
